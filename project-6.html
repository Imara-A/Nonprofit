Career Guidance

Conducting Career Guidance Programmes for Students hwo have completed the Ordinary level Examinations

Students who have faced the Ordinary Level Examinations come to a point where they struggle to decide on their future career. The lack of awareness in about the world of work leads to the inability of choosing the proper subject stream to continue their A/ls. Some students choose subjects that are extremely hard for them, and some choode subjects that wont benifit then for their desired feild of work. Students dont recieve required guidandeand support since most parents as well, do not pocess the knowledge to help their child.

We conducted this project to help students who are lost in their track, by showing a clear path for them and making the future clear.

Proffessionals in many leading feilds were invited to lecture about the benifits and disadvantages of each and every feild of work. The students were given proper knowledge on how to select the correct stream of their need. Apart from that, religeous and cultural knowledge was alsp provided.

The program was completely free of charge, so as to make career advice available for the rich and poor like. The students as well as the parents were pleased with what they had gained at the end of the day.
