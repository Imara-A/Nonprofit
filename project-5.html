Class Fees

Providing Financial Aid to Pay the Cless Fees of Poor children

There are many students in the village of Katugoda, who are talented and dedicated for studies, but are unable to continue education due to economic difficulties faced by their parents. Since most wemen are house-wives, if the child's father is unable to work under any circumstance, the family has no income at all. Most people work for daily wedges to provide themselves with the three meals. People with such economic difficulties face troubles to provide facilities and financial support for their childrens' education. Such students are given valuble opportunities through this program. Mostly we provide this aid for students who are facing Advanced Level exams.

We intend to make education easy and accessible for the ones who consider it as a great burden, and provide opportunities for talented students to drive towords success.

First we inspect the income of selected families and identify students with financial needs. Prority is given to students whose fathers are unable to work. Class fees are paid by the Katugoda Welfare Society for the selected students, untill they face the exams.

Many young telented students have gained great opportunities through our efforts. We have been able to produce many educated individuals in Katugoda through this.
