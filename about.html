About

Katugoda Welfare society was first established in 2017 with the aim of minimizing poverty in the village of Katugoda. The goals of the society has been subjected to changes along with time, adopting to the needs and wants of the people. We have been able to help meny lives through our society, that runs solemnly on donations of non-goverenment institutes and individuals.

Mr. Imran Hameed

The founder and chairman of katugoda welfare society

Katugoda Welfare Society was Founded By Mr. Imran Hameed, who has been involved in social works and charity for a long period. He works in collaboration with the local goverenment institutes and NGO's that work around Katugoda to run the charity services.
