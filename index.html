Who we are

Katugoda Welfare society is a community of social workers who impliment and carry out projects to improve the education for the children from poor families. We have been conducting various educational programs within Katugoda, Galle for about five years. Apart fron that, we also collaborate with the local government institutes, identifying the needy of the area, and providing for them with the aid of donations granted by the central goverenment and numerous other sponsors. Over the years, we have been able to help students with different needs by providing vocational training, and creating a community of self employed individuals.

Our
Mission

Empowering development by deminishing poverty, contributing to improve facilities in the sectors of health and education, forming a healthy, non-toxic environment to create an educated generation for the future of Katugoda.

Types of Projects

EDUCATION

We provide educational programmes to schooling students as well as vocational training to improve work opportinities to the economically unable.

ENVIRONMENT

We fund cleaning campaigns to create a healthy living environment around the area of Katugoda, and beach clean-ups to protect the coast.

CAREER GUIDANCE

We provide vocational training to students who are unable to pay for them, in partership with the local goverenment.
