Scholarship Seminar

Free Seminar for Grade 5 Students who are facing the Scholarship Examination

The grade 5 scholarship exam is a competitive goverenment exam faced by students at the age of 10. Around the area of Katugoda, students facevast difficulties preparing for this important exam of their lives. This is mostly bacause of the inability to pay for tution classes due to economic background of students. These difficulties reduces the tendancy of students to pass the scholarship exam successfully.

This program was aimed to provide practice for students and gain confidence to face this challenging exam.

This was a free seminar and workshop, and was open for any student who is studying in grade 5. The program was conducted by a school teacher with prior experience with preparing students for the scholarship examination.

The program was a satisfaction for all participants as well as our Welfare society. The students truly learned something new, and we intend to conduct more similar programs in the future.
