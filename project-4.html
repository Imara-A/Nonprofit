Dengue Prevention

Conducting Campaigns to Prevent Dengue

Katugoda is named as a danger zone when it comes to spreading of dengue. This is mainly due to the ignorance of the high officials when it comes to concerns of tidyness in the area. Every month, many cases of Dengue infections are being reported. Since there is no medicine for dengue, many deaths are reported as well. The general public face many challanges due to the rapid spread of Dengue.

Thruogh this program, we conduct awareness campaigns around the city to bring attention to this problem. Apart from that, we also conduct clean-ups around the village with the help of the recidents of Katugoda, to maintain the sanity of the area.

Conducting workshops, is one of the most common remedy we use to this problem. We alo give out pamphlets and hang posters in Katugoda. By recruiting the people of Katugoda and the menbers of Katugoda Welfare Society, we conduct shramadhana campaigns from time to time.

The efforts of the Katugoda Welfare Society helps reduce the number of reported dengue casses, as well as keep the environment clean and friendly. The programs we conduct by recruiting villagers also helps them to form a strong bond among each other.
