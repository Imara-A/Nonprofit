Disaster Relief

Providing Food and Other Materials for Areas Affected by Natural Disasters

Katugoda is an area that is mostly affected by floods and landslides, the most often one being floods. The low land areas are freaquently flooded even at the slightest rain. People who work for daily wedges are unable to leave their homes on these days, thus being unable to earn the money required to provide themselves with the food required for the particular day. At some occations, the case is that, some are unable to cook their food since the kitchens are subjected to flooding as well.

The aim of this program is to provide not only food, but also other essencial items to facilitate living for the people who are affected.

The flood affected areas are pre-Identifid by us. At the notice of a risk of flooding, these areas are inspected and necessary steps are taken to provide relief for these people according to paln. The items that are to be distributed are pre-collected and kept in store for emergancies.

Conducting this program has helped people numerous times so far, at times they are strangled in these disasterous situations.
